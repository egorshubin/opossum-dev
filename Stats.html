Product Sales Reports, Statistics in One Table

Stop wasting time using standard instruments to collect statistics. Get access to essential reporting tools and analytics, all from one place. Easily get insight that helps you and your partners make inventory management decisions.
  • See all sales statistics for all suppliers/manufacturers in a single table. There's no need to open each product anymore.
  • Learn about your best market (country), top sales network/payment method, and best product sales by time range.
  • Use various filters: by date, category, supplier/manufacturer, and many more.
  • See the total sales report for a chosen period of time.
  • See sales for separate attribute combinations in a single table.
  • Give access to statistics to your partners - suppliers and manufacturers. It's safe; they will be able to log in and see only statistics for their products. They won't get access to anything else in the admin area (you can also hide stock quantities from them).
  • You and your partners can export sales reports in a CSV file.

So, which main statistics will you and your partners see?

  • Quantities in stock
  • Sales quantities
  • Sales income
  • And much more!
